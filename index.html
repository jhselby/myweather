Sustained Impact
Cumulative stress from continuous wind exposure. Affects dock lines, outdoor furniture anchoring, and prolonged exposure activities. Answers: "Can I hang out outside?"
score = wind_speed × exposure^1.5
Gust Impact
Peak force from wind spikes. Threatens structural integrity, creates sudden loads on dock hardware, and can damage unsecured items. Answers: "Will the house shake?"
